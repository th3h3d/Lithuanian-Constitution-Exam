# Lithuanian Constitution Exam

How to pass
===========

Description:
-----------

Example questions for the exam (from http://konstitucijosegzaminas.lt/bandomasis-egzaminas/) to study Lithuanian Constitution Exam.

Notes:
-----------

Please keep in mind that English version of the questions were translated on Google translate therefore there might be some translation issue.

Suggestions:
-----------

* Read constitution couple times and try you understand the parts of the goverment and their duties.

* Solve questions and study their answers as a topic.

* Look at questions and put down common verbs/words and learn them.

* Solve all questions at least 3 times (you can get exam questions randomly from below link).
